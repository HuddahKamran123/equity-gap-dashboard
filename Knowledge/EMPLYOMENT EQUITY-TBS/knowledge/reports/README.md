## Critical: WFA benchmark changed in 2022–23
Do not compare representation rates across years without checking which WFA was in effect.  
A change in gap-to-WFA between 2021–22 and 2022–23 may reflect the updated census benchmark, not a real policy change.

## What each report contains
- Representation rates for all four designated groups
- WFA benchmarks and gap analysis
- Executive-level (EX) representation
- New hires, promotions, and separations by group
- Policy initiatives and five areas of focus
- Year-over-year commentary from TBS
