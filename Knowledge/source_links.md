## PSES 2024 (Public Service Employee Survey)

**Source file:** EEINFODV.csv — uploaded to project. Public Service Employee Survey 2024 micro-data, broken down by equity group and department. 1,627,293 rows.

**PSES program page**
https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/innovation/public-service-employee-survey.html

**Key equity group codes used in this project:**
- D111A = 1 → Women
- D112 = 1 → Persons with Disabilities
- D113 = 1 → Indigenous Peoples
- D114 = 1 → Members of Visible Minorities

**Subindicators extracted:** Employee engagement, Diversity and inclusion, Harassment, Discrimination, Mobility and retention
